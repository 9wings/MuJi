ABOUT US

This is a Final Group Project for Web Programming (COMP-0020) class at Tufts University taught by Professor Ming Chow. Our team consists of Manish Aryal, Titapa Chaiyakiturajai, Anna Yuen, and Naoki Okada. We are all currently sophomores. With the help of Spotify API and DeepAffects API, MuJi provides you with a song that we deem best fits your current detected mood. We intake your audio and produce an emotion based on your tone and voice.
